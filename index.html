Our Vision

  • To build capacity under our Mission ‘Save Life Around YOU’ by teaching emergency life-saving skills to the community through specially designed program of Emergency Preparedness (E-Prep) and Emergency Response (E Resp)
  • Create a nurturing, stable, culturally diverse and inclusive environment which encourages under-privileged youth to become effective, contributing adults.
  • Provide shelter to homeless, to prevent child abuse and neglect by promoting positive foster parenting, and home away from home where children are valued and loved, educate them, and make them enjoy their childhood.
  • Impart LIVELIHOOD skills to the GIRL/CHILDREN/WOMEN & to enhance sustainability & create economic opportunity
  • Our medical camps shall reach under-served strata; we shall deliver health messages on nutrition, hygiene and sanitation & reduce incidents of threatening life-style diseases.
